Get feedback on your designs in three steps.

### Step 1: Install Floto

Install the Floto plugin from Figma Community.

[Install Floto on Figma](https://www.figma.com/community/plugin/1517405046280643746)

Takes one minute. You'll need it to audit designs and test with personas.

### Step 2: Pick Your First Feature

Choose what feedback you need:

### Step 3: Run Your First Feedback

Open the plugin and click the feature. Select a frame, answer a question or provide a URL, and get results instantly.

Results drop straight onto your canvas — review insights and iterate based on feedback.
