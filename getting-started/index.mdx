## Why Floto

Ship designs users will actually love. From design to validated product in minutes, not weeks.

Floto brings AI audits, synthetic personas, and user interviews directly to your canvas — so you validate designs before developers touch them.

**7k+ designers** already using Floto on Figma and Chrome.
